who we are

Xenophos is the result of the ongoing collaboration between Alex Lenail and Bradford Thorne, and a number of student artists from and around Palo Alto High School in California. Armed with home-made light apparatuses, we've sought to prove that lightpainting is more than just a curiosity but an art in its own right. Meet us...

what we do

Lightpainting is long exposure photography in a dark context with interpretive waving of lights. Cameras typically capture moments with a shutter opening for a fraction of a second, but when that same shutter is opened for much longer spans of time, one image can capture trails of light, brushstrokes to a lightpainter.
